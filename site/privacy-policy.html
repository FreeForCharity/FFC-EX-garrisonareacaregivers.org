Privacy

Garrison Area Caregivers respects the privacy of visitors, volunteers, donors, and community members. Information submitted through email links, Google Forms, Signup.com, Google Drive, or other linked services is handled by those services and may also be received by Garrison Area Caregivers for organizational purposes.

We use information you provide to respond to questions, coordinate volunteers, process donations, maintain records, and improve our services. We do not sell personal information.

Questions may be directed to a board member through the About Us page.
