Volunteer

Volunteers make the difference. Garrison Area Caregivers is run by volunteers, and all operations labor at both Dorothy's Rainbow Food Shelf and Dorothy's Rainbow Thrift Store is volunteer-powered. We can always use your help.

Online signup

Use the links below to open the volunteer calendar for either Dorothy's Rainbow Thrift Store or Dorothy's Rainbow Food Shelf. A name and email address are required to sign up. Your information is not shared outside our organization, and Signup.com will send reminders for the shifts you choose.

Occasionally we may send a request for help with a specific event or time slot that is short-handed. Come and help. It is a great way to get involved, meet people, and build community with others who share the common goal of doing good locally.

Volunteer Calendar

Use the published Signup.com calendar to see upcoming volunteer opportunities in one place. Calendar updates may take time to appear after changes are made in Signup.com.

Food Shelf volunteer times

Food Shelf service runs Tuesdays from 1:00-5:00 pm. Additional signup slots are available for volunteers to help unload and stock truck deliveries. Use Signup.com to see open spots and choose a shift.

Open Food Shelf Volunteer Calendar

Thrift Store volunteer times

Dorothy's Rainbow Thrift Store is open Thursday through Saturday from 10:00 am-4:00 pm. Additional time slots are available to help sort, wash, price, and stock shelves on Tuesdays when the store is not open.

Open Thrift Store Volunteer Calendar

Other ways to help

We are always looking for people to volunteer in other ways too, including maintenance and repairs, serving on the board, or running to town for a specific food order when something is out of stock.

If you are interested in helping with something like this, contact a board member and let them know what you are thinking.
