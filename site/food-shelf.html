Dorothy's Rainbow

Food Shelf

Food support for households in the Garrison area.

Food Shelf hours

Tuesdays from 1:00-5:00 pm at Dorothy's Rainbow in Garrison.

Food sources

Food Shelf items are sourced from Second Harvest, rescue food, garden produce, community donations, and purchased items from Garrison Family Foods, Walmart, Costco, Dollar General, and other local stores.

What is shared

Food and non-food household staples are given out to people in need as available. Special meal items are also provided during the holiday season.
